About Sunshine Driving Centre

Professional, patient and safety-focused driving instruction based in Penshurst, Sydney.

Instructor Profile

Helping Learners Become Safe, Responsible and Confident Drivers

Sunshine Driving Centre is based in Penshurst and provides professional driving lessons across surrounding South Sydney suburbs.

The instructor is an experienced and safety-focused driving professional with over 15 years of driving experience in Australia. The instructor holds a NSW Driving Instructor Licence along with Class C and HR licences.

The main focus is not only helping learners pass the driving test, but also helping them develop safe driving habits, strong road awareness and responsible decision-making.

  • NSW Driving Instructor Licence holder
  • Class C and HR licence holder
  • Over 15 years of Australian driving experience
  • English, Nepali and Hindi spoken fluently

Teaching Approach

More Than Just Passing the Test

Lessons are designed to build confidence, safety awareness and real-world driving skills.

Safety First

Lessons focus on road rules, safe behaviour, hazard awareness and adapting to real traffic conditions.

Patient Instruction

Calm and supportive guidance for nervous learners, beginners and students who need extra confidence.

Multilingual Support

Lessons can be explained clearly in English, Nepali or Hindi to support better understanding.

Test Preparation

Learners receive practical test preparation, mock test support and guidance for test day.

Real Road Experience

Transport Experience That Helps Learners

Throughout the instructor’s career, experience has been gained across professional transport roles including bus driving, rideshare driving, taxi driving and airport valet services.

This background provides strong knowledge of road safety, passenger service, vehicle control and safe driving behaviour in different road conditions.

Every learner progresses at a different pace, so lessons are adjusted according to each student’s confidence, skill level and learning style.
